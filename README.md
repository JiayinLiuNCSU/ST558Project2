# ST558Project2
I choose the US store data set. I'm interested in the question:What factors are related to higher sales and profitability?

Sub-question includes following:

1. Do sales and profit differ across product categories, customer segments, and regions?

2. Is a higher discount associated with lower profit?

3. Does the relationship between sales and profit differ by product category?

4. How do monthly sales and profit change over time?

All the information related to the questions can be found in the DataDescription file. 

In my app, you can check the summary information related to the profit, sales, region and other information. The summary is divided into categorical data summary and numerical data summary. You can not only get the summary statistics there, but also the different type of plots based on the variable you are interested in.

Other than the common summary plot, there are four featured plots included in another tab, with in that, you can see the potential answer for my interest questions. They are easy to interprete, but very helpful.
